ILAHIA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET) is an engineering college located in Muvattupuzha, Kerala. It was established in 2002 and is approved by AICTE and affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU). The college offers undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA) programs in various engineering and technology fields. The campus is spread over 30 acres with modern facilities like smart classrooms, labs, hostels, and a library. ICET focuses on both academics and student development through clubs, workshops, and technical fests. It also has a placement cell that trains students and helps them find job opportunities.
