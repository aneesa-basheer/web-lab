ICET

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET), established in 2002, is a self-financing, co-educational engineering institution located in Muvattupuzha, Kerala. Approved by AICTE and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), ICET offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and computer applications. The college is known for its student-centric approach, modern infrastructure, quality education, and focus on holistic development. With a strong emphasis on academic excellence, innovation, and values, ICET aims to shape competent professionals ready for global challenges.
