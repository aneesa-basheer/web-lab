Department of Computer Application

Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology is a 2-year full-time postgraduate program designed to equip students with advanced knowledge in computer science, programming, and software development. The course blends theory with practical experience through labs, projects, seminars, and industry exposure. Admissions are based on entrance exams, and graduates are prepared for careers in IT such as software development, system analysis, and data management. The program has a strong placement record and industry-focused curriculum.
